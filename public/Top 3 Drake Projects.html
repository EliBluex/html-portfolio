The Best Drake Projects

My top 3 Drake Projects of all time

1. Nothing Was the Same

His greatest project to me. I don't know how many times I have listened to the project as a whole at this point. Undenaibale Classic

2. Take Care

Most of us grew up hearing the many hits on this album. Timeless and unparalleled in terms of the amount of hits on that album. Some would even argue that it is his greatest project

3. If You're Reading This It's Too Late

Nonstop heat on this project. If you haven't heard it yet, I am sorry. This is what Views was supposed to be. Can you imagine if it was??
